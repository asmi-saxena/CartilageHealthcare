## What You Need to Customize

When deploying to your domain, update:

1. **Company Information**
   - Company name
   - Contact details
   - Phone number
   - Email addresses

2. **Images**
   - Hero background
   - Product images
   - Logo/favicon

3. **Content**
   - Service descriptions
   - Product information
   - Team member details
   - Contact addresses

4. **Website URL**
   - Update `next-sitemap.config.js` with your domain
   - Verify metadata titles and descriptions
